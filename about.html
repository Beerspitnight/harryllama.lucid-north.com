About Lucid North

Lucid North is an education-focused technology firm dedicated to building intelligent, human-centered solutions. We believe that the right technology can foster better relationships and create clarity in complex environments. Our commitment is to quality, integrity, and innovative problem-solving.

Our Vision

To empower organizations by transforming their most challenging operational puzzles into simple, elegant workflows. We build tools that give leaders their time back, allowing them to focus on what truly drives growth and success: their people.

Our Flagship Solution: Harry Llama

Harry Llama is the intelligent answer to one of K-12 education's most persistent challenges: substitute scheduling. This AI-powered platform transforms the chaotic '5 AM scramble' into a seamless, automated workflow, empowering administrators to reclaim their day and focus on instructional leadership.
