Educational Leadership Experience

Brian McManus, M.Ed., MA., is an educational leader and EdTech founder with over 20 years of experience dedicated to improving school systems. His career has spanned both U.S. public and international schools, serving for more than a decade as a high school English teacher before moving into administration for ten years with six years as an Assistant Principal.As an administrator, Brian routinely led professional development for over 200 teachers and facilitated nearly 1,000 parent meetings. He is an Evaluation Team Member for the Council of International Schools and served in numerous school accreditation visits for NEASC and MSA.
